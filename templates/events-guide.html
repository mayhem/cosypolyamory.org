⇧ 💜 Official Event 💜

Official Cosy events are published on meetup

Example:
  • Cosy Beach 🏖
Disclaimer

If a purely Cosy group decides to go out to a festival or go dancing or whatever, people who are not in our group haven't signed up to the CoC and we can't follow up on any negative interactions with them.

⇧ ☀️ Cosy Internal Gathering ☀️

Unofficial Cosy gatherings are organised in the Telegram chat by and for known members. Only members and personal guests will attend

Example:
  • "I would like to organise a gathering regarding X in this group"
  • "Anyone in the chat up for X (drinks, dancing, museum etc)?"
Disclaimer:

This is an unoffical Cosy gathering. The Code of conduct still applies. In addition, please bear [external-policies] in mind. Cosy guest policy applies. There may not be an official Cosy Facilitator Team member present. The initiator and present members will follow the CoC.

⇧ 🫧 General Invitation 🫧

Individual invitation for events organised outside of Cosy (public or private)

Example:
  • "I'm going to event X, does any one want to join?"
  • "I'm organising event X, does anyone want to join?"
Disclaimer:

This is not an official Cosy event. Individuals outside our community may be present. We cannot guarantee that all attendees have agreed to our Code of Conduct. As such, we are unable to take responsibility for any behavior that may violate our standards.
